# HereNow Design Style Options

Five visual directions to choose from. Each folder contains reference images.

---

## 1. Warm & Playful (`1-warm-playful/`)
**Inspiration:** BeReal, Locket
- Rounded shapes, warm gradients (peach, coral, soft yellow)
- Bouncy animations, large touch targets
- Friendly sans-serif (Nunito, Poppins)
- Emoji-forward, hand-drawn/organic feeling
- **Best for:** Emphasizing casual, low-pressure nature of HereNow

**References:**
- `bereal-feed-notification.png` — BeReal's daily feed and notification UI
- `bereal-discovery-privacy.png` — BeReal's discovery tab and settings
- `soft-shades-palette.png` — Soft color palette example

---

## 2. Minimal & Bold (`2-minimal-bold/`)
**Inspiration:** Linear, Nothing Phone
- Monochrome or very limited palette (black/white + one accent)
- Strong typography hierarchy, tight spacing
- Sharp or fully rounded corners, no shadows
- Content density and clarity-focused
- **Best for:** Making the availability status feel urgent/prominent

**References:**
- `linear-ui-redesign.png` — Linear's redesigned UI overview
- `linear-before-after.png` — Before/after comparison
- `linear-dark-light-themes.png` — Dark and light theme execution
- `linear-navigation.png` — Navigation patterns
- `monochromatic-scheme.png` — Monochromatic color approach

---

## 3. Soft Glassmorphism (`3-glassmorphism/`)
**Inspiration:** Apple iOS 26 Liquid Glass, Arc Browser
- Frosted glass cards with blur backgrounds, translucency
- Muted pastels with vibrant accent pops
- SF Pro or Inter for native feel
- Subtle depth via layered blur + thin borders
- **Best for:** Feeling premium and native on iOS

**References:**
- `apple-liquid-glass-hero.jpg` — Apple's Liquid Glass design language overview
- `apple-liquid-glass-ios.jpg` — iOS 26 Liquid Glass in action
- `apple-liquid-glass-controls.jpg` — Glassmorphic controls and buttons

## 4. Warm Neutral / Neo-Organic (`4-warm-neutral-organic/`)
**Inspiration:** Calm, Headspace
- Earthy tones: cream, sage green, warm grays, terracotta
- Rounded "blob" shapes, soft illustrations
- Serif or humanist sans-serif headers (DM Sans, Fraunces)
- Feels human and intentional vs. techy
- **Best for:** Reinforcing the "real human connection" philosophy

## 5. Vibrant & High-Energy (`5-vibrant-high-energy/`)
**Inspiration:** Cash App, Duolingo, Cleo
- Bold, saturated primary color (lime green, electric blue, deep purple)
- High contrast, chunky buttons, thick borders
- Playful micro-interactions and haptic feedback
- Heavy use of motion (spring animations, scale on press)
- **Best for:** Making "I'm available" feel exciting and rewarding
